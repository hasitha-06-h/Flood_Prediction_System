No Significant Flooding Expected

Based on the readings submitted, the model does not currently indicate a high flood probability for this area. Conditions can still change quickly during monsoon season, so it's worth staying prepared regardless.

General Preparedness Tips
  • Keep a basic emergency kit ready regardless of current risk level.
  • Check that drainage around your property is clear of debris before monsoon season.
  • Save your local disaster helpline number in your phone.
  • Re-check the forecast periodically, especially after sustained heavy rainfall.
When to Re-Check
  • If rainfall in your area increases noticeably over the next 24-48 hours.
  • If nearby rivers, dams or reservoirs approach warning levels.
  • If local authorities issue a weather advisory or watch for your district.
  • Before making travel plans through low-lying or flood-prone routes.
